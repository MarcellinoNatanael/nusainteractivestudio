Demit

Release: June 07, 2024

Nusa Interactive Studio

HorrorSingle PlayerPsychological Horror
Looping

A student named Henry migrated from Jakarta to Bandung and is now looking for a cheap place to live. He looked here and there until finally he saw a flyer that there was an apartment not far from here. This apartment is an apartment left over from the Dutch colonial era and it is rare for someone to want to stay there because of its awesomeness. Hearing the story, Henry didn't pay attention to it because he himself didn't believe in mystical things, until finally he found out all about the dark history of the apartment so that no one wanted to stay there. For 10 days, Henry will be terrorized by a scary figure who will scare Henry throughout the game.

Perjanjian Gaib

Release: May 29, 2025

Nusa Interactive Studio

HorrorSingle PlayerPsychological HorrorStory RichLow Poly

The Occult Agreement is a first-person, single-player narrative horror game. The story follows Dirga, a student from Bandung who received a scholarship to Jakarta. Lately, Dirga has been feeling uneasy about his family. One day, the neighborhood head (Paduk RT) from his village reports that Dirga's family has been behaving strangely and isolating themselves from the local community. The Pak RT advises Dirga to return home and check on his family. Upon arriving home, Dirga discovers a dark family secret: his grandfather was once a renowned psychic who not only healed but also spread black magic. Thirsting for supernatural power, his grandfather made a pact with the devil, who then threatened Dirga's family descendants one by one. To break the curse, Dirga's grandfather left a message that anyone who found a necklace in the secret room must burn it.
